Childhood Years

            I can still remember my childhood as happy and as nostalgic as it is.1 It was the best time of my life where I only had to worry about playing and having more friends.2 Well before, making friends was much easier.3 I remember how I used to be friends with most of the other kids from my neighborhood and we would play outside until sunset.4 My life before during school year is only divided into school life and after school life.5 Wherein, after school is where I would go outside even though the sun is still high.6 I didn’t worry about getting tanned nor getting dirty from too much playing in the field as long as I spend my time with my friends.7 But, the best part is yet to come.8 And, it is summer where we get to spend our vacation at my mother’s family house in their province for almost two months.9 My cousins who are close to our age became our closest companions before during our entire childhood. 10 Ever since babies we’ve been introduced by our parents. 11 Oh! how much memories we have created together, from wearing diapers to playing in the plaza.12 What makes it more happier is that their house is only one walk away from the street. 13 Everything that a child could wish of is already there. 14 Both of our family houses have gardens where we can play with our cousins and aside from that, my mother’s old family house became like a farm.15 My tito had chickens and crops of different kinds.16 He also had different kinds of birds before like lovebirds, java birds and martinez.17 And, my grandmother had a garden of different plants.18 How fun it was to chase the chickens before and to also feed them and watch them grow.19 Then, in the afternoon we would go to the plaza which is only walking distance to buy our favorite meryenda, Empanada, Ilocos’ specialty.20 And, we would go to the Sta.Maria Church near the plaza because we would race climbing it up which has more than a hundred stairs.21 It's considered a tourist spot of our municipality and is inscribed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.22

Teenage Years

            As for my teenage years and high school life, it was more focused on studies where I managed to continuously excel academically.1 But, also the stage of my life where we had to say goodbye to my childhood friends from the neighborhood.2 It is because we moved into a new home and thus a new school.3 I did have friends from the school except for the neighborhood.4 The new apartment was full of adult and younger kids.5 We didn’t have neighbors who are close to our age.6 But, we still have our childhood cousins.7 Even in our high school time, we would still spend out two months vacation in my mother’s family house. 8 It became like a tradition.9 It is also in my teenage years where my friends from my school became also my friends after school.10 It was a new chapter in my life.11 New experiences, new environment, new changes and many more. 12 It was the time wherein most of us have crushes and have interest and curiosity in relationships.13 It was also the time in cultivating talents and skills.14 And, choosing the course and university you would like to go to.15 Of course, mostly would want to be on the top universities which is why almost everyone are preparing for the entrance exams and fulfilling their university's requirements.16 It is the time of choosing whether to follow what you want or what your parents want.17 As for me, I chose what my parents wanted.18 And, so I prepared for the entrance exam of the universities’ they preferred, which are state universities.19 However, I only passed in one school where their preference of course is not available.20 And so, we were left with private universities.21

College Years

            Starting my college years was tiring.1 A new chapter in my life again where everything became much more serious.2 My mother wanted me to pursue nursing and so I tried it.3 It was tiring to be in a top medical university as they would really push you to your limits.4 It was a never ending study of thick books with small writings.5 I remember how our professor used to say that in their university, it is a sin to sleep for more than 4 hours.6 And, he was right.7 Though, those sleepless nights and breakdown from memorizing thick books paid off when I was able to be a dean’s lister, but never have I ever thought that I would be able to make it.8 However, my life in nursing didn’t last long because my parents can no longer afford the tuition fees during the second semester.9 And, so I stopped for almost half a year.10 During that time, I decided to shift course and change school which brings me here to UE. 11 The college life in Manila was very different from my previous experience.12 Moreover, I became an irregular student because my minor subjects were credited.13 I’m still holding on to this course for a better future.14 I believe that it will be rewarded soon.15 College years taught me that life is not a race.16 And, I should not be defined by my grades but on how much I learned.17 It was the biggest change in my life as I’m no longer putting my value as a person in my class standing but rather by my experiences in life and how I value myself. 18 In this chapter of my life, I wanted to focus on myself and to improve it physically, mentally and emotionally.19 It may be tiring but I accept what life has to offer — may it be good or bad.20 I’ll just take the negative as a lesson.21
